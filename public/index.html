About Kalra Travels

Since 2013, Kalra Travels has been making journeys safe, comfortable, and unforgettable. Serving Kurukshetra, Ambala, Chandigarh, Yamunanagar, Pehowa, Ladwa & Shahabad, we offer 24x7 Airport Pick & Drop and One-Way Services.

From Ertiga & Dzire to premium rides like Audi & Creta, our fleet is ready for every trip — whether it’s a scenic tour or a special occasion.

Experience the Breathtaking Beauty of the Himalayas

Embark on a journey with Kalra Travels and uncover the true essence of the Himalayas. Traverse the Great Himalayan Range and marvel at the stunning diversity — from lush valleys teeming with wildlife to meadows blooming with rare orchids, and the rugged Trans Himalaya stretching towards the Tibetan plateau. This magical journey will gift you unforgettable memories of majestic mountains and unique landscapes.

Connect with Local Communities

As you explore the Himalayas, engage with warm-hearted local communities. Discover their traditional ways of life and immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Witness Rare Wildlife and Flora

The Himalayas are home to endangered species like the snow leopard, red panda, and blue sheep. Keep your eyes open for these remarkable creatures as you journey through diverse ecosystems.

Get Ready for the Adventure of a Lifetime

Whether you’re a trekker, nature enthusiast, or seeking a one-of-a-kind travel experience, the Himalayas have something for everyone. Start planning your adventure with Kalra Travels today!
